How Gas Cost is Calculated

The gas cost calculation follows these steps:

  1. Gas Amount: The number of gas units your transaction requires (e.g., 21000 for a basic transfer)
  2. Gas Price: Current network price in Gwei (e.g., 32 Gwei)
  3. Convert to ETH:
    (Gas × Gwei) ÷ 1,000,000,000 = ETH
    or
    (Gas × wei) ÷ 1,000,000,000,000,000 = ETH
    Example: (21000 × 32) ÷ 1,000,000,000 = 0.000672 ETH
  4. Convert to USD:
    ETH × ETH Price = USD
    Example: 0.000672 × $3,300 = $2.22

Note: The displayed cost uses the "Average" gas price tier.
